Feijoada

Ingredients

Directions

  1. 4 ounces diced bacon
  2. 1 pound boneless pork shoulder, cut into 1 inch chunks
  3. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  4. 1 pound boneless beef short ribs, cut into 1 inch chunks (or carne seca - salted beef - if you can find it)
  5. ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  6. 1 pound smoked sausage (linguica sausage if you can find it)
  7. 1 large onion, diced
  8. 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  9. ½ teaspoon kosher salt or ¼ teaspoon table salt
  10. 1 pound (2 ¼ cups) dried black beans, sorted and rinsed
  11. ½ teaspoon baking soda
  12. 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  13. 5 cups water
  14. 2 bay leaves
  15. Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  16. Minced fresh parsley (optional)
